## 🚨 CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

1. **Fast Response Time:** Respond to leads within 2 hours
2. **High-Quality Content:** Professional ad creative and landing pages
3. **Clear Value Proposition:** Focus on expertise, not price
4. **Consistent Monitoring:** Daily campaign checks
5. **Data-Driven Decisions:** Base optimizations on metrics, not assumptions

---

## ⚠️ COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

1. **Don't:** Launch all campaigns at once without testing
2. **Don't:** Ignore low-quality leads (wastes budget)
3. **Don't:** Set and forget campaigns (needs daily monitoring)
4. **Don't:** Use generic messaging (must target high-end clients)
5. **Don't:** Skip follow-up automation (leads go cold quickly)
