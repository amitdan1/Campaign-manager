# Performance Marketing Strategy for Ofir Assulin Interior Design
## Target: High-End Clients in Central Israel

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Business Goal:** Maximize lead generation for wealthy clients building houses from scratch or renovating villas with high budgets in central Israel.

**Target Audience:** 
- Age: 35-55
- Location: Central Israel (Tel Aviv, Herzliya, Ramat Hasharon, Kfar Shmaryahu, Savyon, etc.)
- Income: High net worth individuals
- Life Stage: Married professionals with families
- Pain Point: Overwhelmed by renovation decisions, need expert guidance
- Budget: ₪500K+ for interior design projects

**Key Insight:** High-end clients start with Google search ("עיצוב פנים הרצליה", "מעצבת פנים וילה") and need trust-building before conversion. They value expertise, not price.

---

## MARKET ANALYSIS

### Competitive Landscape (Israel)
- **Leading designers** use Facebook/Instagram but rely heavily on Google Ads
- **Average CPL (Cost Per Lead):** ₪200-500 for high-end segment
- **Conversion rate:** 2-5% from lead to consultation, 30-50% consultation to project
- **Average project value:** ₪300K-1M+

### What Works in Israeli Market
1. **Google Ads** - Primary channel for warm/hot leads searching actively
2. **Instagram/Facebook** - Brand awareness and portfolio showcase
3. **Lead Magnets** - Free consultations, design guides, questionnaires
4. **Retargeting** - Critical for high-ticket sales (7-12 touchpoints needed)

### What Doesn't Work
- Generic portfolio posts without targeting
- Price-focused messaging (attracts wrong clients)
- Organic-only social media (5-7% engagement rate)
- One-time campaigns (needs consistent presence)

## PERFORMANCE MARKETING CHANNELS

### 1. GOOGLE ADS (Primary Channel)
**Budget Allocation:** 50-60% of total ad spend

**Campaign Types:**
- **Search Campaigns** (Hebrew keywords):
  - "עיצוב פנים הרצליה"
  - "מעצבת פנים וילה"
  - "עיצוב פנים בתים פרטיים"
  - "מעצבת פנים רמת השרון"
  - "עיצוב פנים בתים חדשים"
  - "מעצבת פנים בתל אביב"

- **Display/YouTube Retargeting:**
  - Target website visitors
  - Video portfolio showcases
  - Before/after transformations

**Expected Metrics:**
- CPC: ₪15-40 (high competition in central Israel)
- CPL: ₪300-600
- Monthly budget: ₪15,000-30,000
- Leads/month: 30-60 qualified leads

### 2. FACEBOOK & INSTAGRAM ADS (Secondary Channel)
**Budget Allocation:** 30-40% of total ad spend

**Campaign Strategy:**
- **Lead Generation Campaigns:**
  - Native Lead Forms (lower friction)
  - Target: 35-55, Central Israel, interests: luxury brands, real estate, architecture
  - Lookalike audiences from website visitors/email list

- **Video Portfolio Ads:**
  - Before/after transformations
  - Behind-the-scenes content
  - Client testimonials

**Expected Metrics:**
- CPL: ₪200-400
- Monthly budget: ₪10,000-20,000
- Leads/month: 25-50 leads

### 3. RETARGETING (Critical for Conversion)
**Budget Allocation:** 10-20% of total ad spend

**Strategy:**
- Website visitors (all pages)
- Video viewers (75%+ watched)
- Lead form abandoners
- Email subscribers who haven't booked consultation

**Messaging:**
- Social proof (testimonials, awards)
- Limited-time consultation offers
- Case studies matching their interests

## CONVERSION FUNNEL

### Stage 1: AWARENESS
- **Channel:** Facebook/Instagram ads, Google Display
- **Goal:** Brand awareness, portfolio showcase
- **CTA:** "View Portfolio" or "Get Free Guide"

### Stage 2: CONSIDERATION
- **Channel:** Google Search, Retargeting
- **Goal:** Capture intent, provide value
- **CTA:** "Download Free Guide" or "Book Consultation"

### Stage 3: DECISION
- **Channel:** Email, WhatsApp, Retargeting
- **Goal:** Convert to consultation
- **CTA:** "Schedule Free Consultation Call"

### Stage 4: CONVERSION
- **Channel:** Direct contact, consultation follow-up
- **Goal:** Close project
- **Process:** Consultation → Proposal → Contract

## BUDGET ALLOCATION (Monthly)

### Current Budget: ₪500/month

**Budget Allocation:**
1. **Google Ads:** ₪300/month (60%) - High-intent search keywords
   - Focus on 5-8 high-intent keywords
   - Long-tail Hebrew keywords for lower CPC
   - Geographic targeting: central Israel only

2. **Facebook/Instagram:** ₪200/month (40%) - Targeted lead gen
   - Lead generation campaigns with native forms
   - Portfolio showcase and video ads
   - Lookalike audiences from website visitors

**Additional Costs (Tools & AI):**
- **OpenAI API:** ~₪20-40/month for AI-powered agents
- **SendGrid:** Free tier (100 emails/day)
- **Twilio WhatsApp:** ~₪20/month

**Budget Optimization Strategy:**
- Focus on **quality over quantity** - Target only highest-intent keywords
- Use **long-tail keywords** (lower CPC, higher conversion)
- **Geographic targeting** - Only central Israel (Herzliya, Tel Aviv, Ramat Hasharon)
- **Time-based bidding** - Run ads only during peak hours
- **Ad scheduling** - Focus budget on best-performing days
- **Manual bidding** - Control costs precisely
- **AI-powered optimization** - Use agents to continuously improve targeting and messaging

**Expected Results with ₪500/month:**
- 8-15 leads/month
- Focus on **conversion rate optimization** - Higher quality leads
- Target: 40-50% consultation booking rate
- Target: 50%+ project conversion rate
- **ROI Focus:** Even 1 project/month (₪100K+) = 200X+ ROI
